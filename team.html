Who We Are

At Sewing Machines Inc., we pride ourselves on the dedication and expertise of our team, are united by a shared commitment to quality and innovation. Our employees bring a diverse range of skills, knowledge, and experiences to the table, working together seamlessly to design, manufacture, and deliver some of the finest sewing machines in the industry. Our organization is structured into specialized departments, each with a clear focus: our Research and Development team drives product innovation, our Marketing team connects with sewing enthusiasts worldwide, our Sales and Customer Support teams ensure a smooth and satisfying customer experience, and our Executive Leadership guides our mission with strategic vision and passion. Every team member plays an integral role in our mission to provide exceptional products and experiences to our customers, reflecting our dedication to quality and service excellence.

Our Story

Sewing Machines Inc. was established in 1995 with a strong commitment to crafting high-quality, dependable sewing machines for enthusiasts around the world. What began as a small operation with a singular vision has grown over the years into a dynamic, customer-focused company known for both innovation and reliability. From our first product line to our latest advanced models, we have continuously expanded and refined our offerings to meet the evolving needs of the sewing community. Our growth has been fueled by a team of dedicated professionals who share a deep passion for creativity, precision, and customer satisfaction. Today, we are proud to serve a loyal and diverse customer base,delivering products that combine traditional craftsmanship with cutting-edge technology
